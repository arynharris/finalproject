Voice to Vote Project

In this project, Four other group members and I designed a CUI to answer common voter and voter registration questions.

We first researched common voting statistics and decided that our target users would be people that are not registered to vote.

We then created our user personas.

We decided what our voice and tone guidelines would be, meaning how we want our app voice to sound and the tone it should use.

Lastly, we created our CUI by deciding its' entities(words uttered together that trigger the CUI answers) and we made a prototype in Adobe XD
